==========
Binomials
==========

Binomials are the product or difference of two monomials. Let's see 
if there are any special tricks.

Simple Binomials Form
----------------------

Binomials look like either of the following lines:

.. rst-class:: prefix

    + * A B * C D
    
    - * A B * C D

I
